Professional Experience & Expertise

I'm Anna Laevskaya, a certified Myofascial Massage Therapist with extensive training in therapeutic bodywork and pain management. I specialize in myofascial release techniques that target the connective tissue surrounding muscles to restore mobility and alleviate chronic pain.

My practice focuses on understanding each client's unique needs and developing personalized treatment plans that address the root causes of discomfort and movement restrictions.

My Services

Specialized myofascial treatments tailored to your needs

Myofascial Release

Gentle sustained pressure applied to myofascial connective tissue restrictions to eliminate pain and restore motion.

  • Direct myofascial release
  • Indirect myofascial release
  • Structural integration

Trigger Point Therapy

Targeted treatment of hyperirritable spots in skeletal muscle that cause referred pain and muscle dysfunction.

  • Trigger point identification
  • Pressure point release
  • Muscle tension relief

Pain Management

Comprehensive approach to chronic pain relief through specialized massage techniques and movement therapy.

  • Chronic pain assessment
  • Therapeutic massage
  • Movement re-education

Sports Recovery

Specialized treatments for athletes focused on injury prevention, performance enhancement, and recovery.

  • Pre-event preparation
  • Post-event recovery
  • Injury rehabilitation

Postural Correction

Stress Relief
